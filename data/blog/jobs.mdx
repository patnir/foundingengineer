## Step 1: Identify Your Interests

The first step in finding a startup job is to identify industries that genuinely excite you. Passion and curiosity are crucial in the startup world. Not only should you express your interest, but you should also be able to prove it. Here‚Äôs how:

- **Work Experience:** Leverage any relevant work experience.
- **Side Projects:** Engage in side projects related to the industry.
- **Networking:** Attend conferences and meetups to make valuable connections.

**Note:** The best startups provide you the opportunity to grow way beyond your job description. The best founders want you to level up quickly and also lean towards hiring someone with passion over experience especially at the early stages. If you show insane hunger and curiosity about what they are working on, they will at the very least keep you in mind for other opportunities at the company as it grows.

- [Wellfound/ Angellist](https://wellfound.com/): Startup focused job board
